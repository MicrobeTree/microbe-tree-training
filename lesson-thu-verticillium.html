🧾 Quick Reference — Verticillium Wilt

Pathogen
Verticillium dahliae (primary); V. albo-atrum (less common in trees)
Type
Soil-borne ascomycete fungus; systemic xylem pathogen
Survival structure
Microsclerotia — persist in soil 10–15+ years without host
Infection route
Roots (wounds, natural openings) → colonises xylem vessels
One-sided flagging; olive/brown vascular discoloration in cross-section
Susceptible genera
Acer, Ulmus, Fraxinus, Robinia, Cotinus, Prunus, Catalpa, many others
