How trees open, close, and negotiate the carbon–water tradeoff

📐 Quick Reference — Stomatal Mechanics

Primary openerBlue light → H⁺-ATPase pumps → K⁺ influx
Primary closerABA → SnRK2 kinases → anion + K⁺ efflux
VPD effectHigh VPD (>3 kPa) → partial closure regardless of light
DistributionMost broadleaf trees: hypostomatous (lower surface)
ConifersStomatal crypts on needles — reduces boundary layer exchange
Carbon tradeoffEvery CO₂ in = ~300–500 H₂O out under high VPD
