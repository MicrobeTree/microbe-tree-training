Week 1 · Cytospora Canker — Diagnosis, Biology, and PHC in the Okanagan

📋 Quick Reference — Cytospora Canker

Causal agents
Leucostoma kunzei (spruces); L. cincta, L. persoonii (stone fruit, poplar); multiple Cytospora spp.
Primary hosts in Okanagan
Colorado blue spruce, Norway spruce, stone fruit (cherry, peach, plum), poplars, willows
Disease type
Fungal canker — opportunistic pathogen; stress-dependent
Spread vector
Wind and water-dispersed conidia (asexual spores) entering through wounds, sunscald, drought cracks
Key field sign
Resin-soaked, sunken canker at branch base; white to amber resin ooze; blue-gray stain in underlying wood
PHC approach
Reduce stress; improve site conditions; prune dead branches; no chemical cure exists
